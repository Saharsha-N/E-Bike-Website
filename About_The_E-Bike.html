The Urban Version

The Urban TYKO E-Bike is an e-bike created for casual but infrequent use, such as going to the park for a ride... . It has good battery life capable of delivering 35 minutes of run time on its max speed of 22 mph. It can handle a moderate amount of terrains, but caution is required when or if it's ridden with potholes. It comes in a variety of colors: black, yellow, orange, purple, white, red, blue, green, and colors of your own choosing.

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The Deluxe Version

The Deluxe TYKO E-Bike is an e-bike for frequent usage and high travel distances, such as traveling back and forth for errands.... . Compared to the Urban E-Bike, it has a considerably improved battery and a more efficient motor, resulting in its high battery life and mileage. It comes in a variety of colors: black, yellow, orange, purple, white, red, blue, green, and colors of your own choosing.

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The Supreme Version

The Supreme TYKO E-bike is an e-bike for daily use and extreme distances, such as cross-state travels and long distance journeys... . The supreme E-bike can travel on almost any terrain, being as efficient as an ATV when doing so. The Supreme E-bike can travel very long distances without breaks, thanks to its superior battery and motor. It also comes in a variety of colors including custom colors.
