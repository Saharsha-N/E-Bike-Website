The Urban Version

The Urban TYKO E-Bike is an e-bike created for casual but infrequent use, such as going to the park for a stroll or hanging out with friends. It has a good battery life capable of delivering 35 minutes of run time on its max speed of 22 mph. It can handle a moderate amount of terrains, but caution is required when the terrain has holes or if it's ridden with potholes. It comes in a variety of colors including black, yellow, orange, purple, white, red, blue, green, and colors of your own choosing.

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The Deluxe Version

The Deluxe TYKO E-Bike is an e-bike for frequent usage and high travel distances, such as traveling back and forth for errands or dropping friends off. Compared to the Urban E-Bike, it has a considerably improved battery and a more efficient motors, resulting in its high battery life and mileage. It also has the same selection of colors as all the other variations of the E-Bike. It comes in a variety of colors including black, yellow, orange, purple, white, red, blue, green, and colors of your own choosing.

_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The Supreme Version

The Supreme TYKO E-bike is an e-bike for daily use and extreme distances, such as cross-state travels and long distance journeys. The supreme E-bike can travel on almost any terrain, being as efficient as an ATV when doing so. The Supreme E-bike can travel very long distances without breaks with its superior battery and motors. It comes in a variety of colors including black, yellow, orange, purple, white, red, blue, green, and colors of your own choosing.

Environmental Impact

The E-Bike, the product on which this company was founded on, is a completely green and eco-friendly vehicle. The E-bike is a method of transportation which solely relies on electric energy. In comaparison to gasoline-powered vehicles, the E-Bike has almost no detrimental effect on the environment.
